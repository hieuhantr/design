WORK ABOUT

AWS Activate

Structured content and prototyped a landing page for AWS Activate program

SUMMARY

Problem: AWS is currently using email to send Providers all welcoming information; however, it is lengthy, has low readibility, and low engagement.

Solution: A post-click email landing page that equips Providers with all AWS Activate information, so they can start instructing and promoting to associated startups.

My role: Planning, content strategy, wireframing, and prototyping
Project type: Client project; internal team of 3
Tools used: AdobeXD, LucidChart, InvisionApp

MY EXPERIENCE

In this project, I worked with a Strategist and Account Manager to conceptualize and build a landing page, which also acts as an information hub for AWS Providers. I got to work with AWS's design system, modules, and was constrained in the scope and time of the project.

Nonetheless, it was a very collaborative and insightful experience, as I worked alongside the client and engineers to hand over the final design.

Due to the confidentiality of my work with AWS and April Six, I cannot publicly share information or visuals regarding the product that I have worked on. Please contact me for more information or feel free to view my other projects.
