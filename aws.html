Background

Activate Program

Amazon Web Services (AWS) Activate is a free program that works with startups and entrepreneurs to launch, grow, and scale their businesses with AWS. In this program, participating businesses receive free startups tools, resources, training, technical support, and more to quickly get started.

Venture capital firms, accelerators, angel investors, incubators, or other startup-enabling organizations are eligible to apply to become an AWS Activate Provider. Once Providers have a Activate Organization ID, associated startups can apply for the AWS Activate Portfolio to start receiving exclusive benefits designed to help startups quickly accelerate.
